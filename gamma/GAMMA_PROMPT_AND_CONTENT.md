## 2. WHO IS TECHBRIDGE 🌉

### **About TechBridge**
- **Mission:** Connecting nonprofits with technology solutions to amplify their impact
- **Focus:** Bridging the digital divide for underserved communities
- **Georgia-based:** Serving nonprofits across the Southeast
- **Impact:** 25+ years of technology innovation for social good

### **The Challenge:**
TechBridge manages complex food distribution networks, with advanced transactional systems, but lacked advanced dashboards that provide insights to optimize operations and measure impact effectively.

---

## 3. BUSINESS QUESTION & PROJECT OBJECTIVE 🎯

### **Core Challenge:**
*"How can we transform fragmented food distribution data into actionable insights that maximize food security impact?"*

### **Specific Objectives:**
1. **Visibility:** Understand donation flows from donors to recipients
2. **Optimization:** Identify geographic gaps and distribution inefficiencies  
3. **Impact Measurement:** Quantify food security outcomes 

### **Success Metrics:**
- Real-time visibility into food distribution networks
- Data-driven recommendations for operational improvements
- Measurable increase in food distribution efficiency

---

## 4. SOLUTION ARCHITECTURE 🚀

### **Technology Stack:**

#### **Data Infrastructure:**
- **Oracle Database Integration:** Connected to live production systems
- **Python ETL Pipeline:** Automated data extraction and transformation
- **Real-time Processing:** Live data feeds for current insights

#### **Analytics Platform:**
- **Streamlit Dashboard:** Executive-level KPI monitoring and geographic analytics
- **Dash Dashboard:** Interactive operational analytics and detailed insights
- **AI-Powered Visualizations:** Sankey diagrams, choropleth maps, predictive models

#### **Key Features:**
- **Geographic Analytics:** State-level food distribution mapping
- **Donor Performance Tracking:** Real-time donation flow analysis  
- **Storage Optimization:** Refrigerated vs. shelf-stable distribution insights
